    ASL VISION

    Sign languages are widely used by deaf and hard of hearing individuals globally as their primary mode of communication. To bridge the communication gap between the hearing impaired and others, sign language interpreters convert sign language into spoken words and vice versa. Despite the flexible structure of sign languages, the lack of qualified sign language interpreters worldwide poses recruitment difficulties. To address this challenge, a solution has been proposed in the form of an alphabet sign language recognition system. This system aims to enable direct communication with deaf-mute individuals without requiring a qualified sign language interpreter.



    Why Us

    ASL Vision - assists individuals and society in breaking down communication barriers and improving access to information and opportunities for people with hearing impairments, ultimately leading to a more equitable and just society.
