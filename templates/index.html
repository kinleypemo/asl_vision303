INSTRUCTIONS


1. In order to make accurate predictions, it is important to be familiar with the hand signs representing all the alphabets, except for the letters J and Z.

2. The exclusion of the letters J and Z is due to the fact that they require dynamic data, whereas the model has been trained solely on static image datasets.

3. Please refer to the image provided alongside for a visual representation of the hand signs.

4. To proceed with the prediction, please click on the "Predict" button located below.

5. Upon clicking the "Predict" button, a pop-up window will appear, activating your webcam.

6. Once your webcam is active, please position your hand to display the hand sign corresponding to the alphabet you wish to detect.

7.The predicted result for the hand sign you show will be displayed directly above your hand on the screen.

8. Additionally, there is an alternative method for predicting the hand sign. You can upload images of the hand sign directly and click the "Predict" button to obtain the corresponding result.

Note: Only one hand will be detected.
